CCOH: Collect Candy On Halloween

The official wiki page for the Roblox game "CCOH"

Overview

"CCOH" is an exciting Roblox Halloween game where players collect candy, buy costumes, and face various challenges. The goal is to become the top candy collector and unlock a variety of costumes that grant special abilities.

Features

  • Collect candy as in-game currency
  • Explore various Halloween-themed areas
  • Purchase unique costumes with special powers
  • Interact with NPCs and other players
  • Individual sound effects for player interactions
  • Local and global leaderboards for the top candy collectors

Gameplay

In "CCOH", players aim to collect as much candy as possible. You can go from house to house, ringing doorbells to get candy. These candies can be spent in the shop to unlock costumes, each offering unique abilities. Every doorbell click triggers a unique sound that only the player can hear.

Shop

The CCOH shop offers a selection of costumes that can be purchased with collected candy. These costumes change the player's appearance and provide various advantages during gameplay.

  • Pirate Costume - 100 candies
  • Zombie Costume - 500 candies
  • Witch Costume - 750 candies
  • Vampire Costume - 950 candies
  • Ghost Costume - 1000 candies

Developers

The game "CCOH" was developed by Domlixflix, a dedicated Roblox developer aiming to create a unique and fun Halloween experience for the Roblox community. Combining adventure with competition, CCOH offers a thrilling experience for players worldwide.
